Delphin University is a four year college focused on practical academics. Students learn from a variety of offered degrees and programs, focusing on the applications in need, such as computer science, software engineering, and even to areas such as welding and mechanical engineering. Students learn to develop important life habits and skills, while being able to contribute to the community and campus in meaningful ways. We also offer a competitive sports environment, while helping ensure our athletes maintain good academic and social standings. Come and learn more about what Delpin University can offer you.

Sports

At Delphin University we offer a wide variety of sports programs, meant to complement the rigorous academic training also supplied. Those who wish can compete against other top ranking schools in lacrosse, basketball, football, and soccer. As well as competitive sports, also provided are sports for recreational purposes with free equipment and fields for student use.
